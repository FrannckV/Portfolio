Honeymoon

  • Device: iPhone, iPad
  • Framework: SwiftUI
  • Project URL: https://github.com/FrannckV/Honeymoon

Gorgeous iOS 13 and iPadOS app with a Tinder-like user interface design. Since swipe motion gestures with SwiftUI. The app presents users with a pile of Honeymoon destination cards and allows them to use the swipe gesture to like or dislike a card.

  1. Advanced iOS and iPadOS application
  2. Reusable UI components
  3. Eye-catchy app Guide and Info views
  4. The file structures properly organized
  5. Deck of cards
  6. Master long press gestures with SwiftUI
  7. Displays the Heart and X-mark symbols during the swiping
  8. Custom transitions
  9. Removes and inserts the cards on loop
